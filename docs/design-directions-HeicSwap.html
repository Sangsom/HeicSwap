Six ways HeicSwap could look

HeicSwap · 2026-06-19 · ios-product-team · pick 1–2 before the full spec

HeicSwap is a fast, on-device, ad-free image converter. The same Convert screen is rendered six ways below — six different personalities, not one theme in six tints. Each varies the palette (light + dark), the type pairing, the shape language, and one signature move. They split into two camps: those that win on trust (calm, private, premium) and those that win on screenshot CTR (bold, warm, playful).

Tied to the competitor teardown Today's ranking converters look the same: a generic blue-and-white utility, often ad-cluttered, several built on cloud upload. Our wedge is privacy + honesty + speed. The directions that lean into that (Privacy Vault, Quiet Utility) make screenshot #1 — “nothing leaves your iPhone” — land hardest; the louder ones (Bold Swap) win the tap in a grid of pastel sameness.

Recommendation

Recommended: 02 — Privacy Vault Our single most ownable advantage over the two ranking incumbents is on-device privacy — both leaders upload to a server or run on ads. A premium-dark, trust-signaling identity makes that wedge the visual story, so App Store screenshot #1 (“nothing leaves your iPhone”) carries the listing. The mint accent doubles as the universal “go / done” color for a fast utility.

Strong pairing: blend 02 Vault’s dark trust hero with 01 Quiet Utility’s restraint for the light-mode variant — premium where it counts, calm everywhere else. If maximizing install-screenshot CTR is the priority instead, 03 Bold Swap is the alternative.

Pick 1–2 directions and I’ll build the full design spec — flows, screen inventory, interaction + haptics, the complete token system, and accessibility — on the winner. (My pick: 02 Privacy Vault, with 01’s restraint for light mode.)
