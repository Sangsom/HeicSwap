## 1. What This App Does

### One-Liner
On-device HEIC & image converter

### Description
HeicSwap converts HEIC/PNG/JPG images, builds multi-page PDFs, resizes/compresses, and strips EXIF/GPS metadata — entirely **on-device, with zero network egress**. Nothing is ever uploaded; privacy is the core promise.

### Target Audience
People who need to convert iPhone HEIC photos to shareable formats (JPG/PNG/PDF) and who care that their images never leave the device.

### Dependencies
| Package | Purpose | Risk |
|---------|---------|------|
| RevenueCat (purchases-ios 5.79.0) | Subscriptions / paywall | Low |
| TelemetryDeck (SwiftSDK 2.14.1) | Privacy-first analytics | Low |

> The only two third-party SDKs, both pinned to the current major (`upToNextMajorVersion`). ## 5. Monetization

### Current Model
Subscription + lifetime (hard paywall model, value-gated). Plumbing built in 6.1 (offerings fetch, purchase/restore, offline-cached `EntitlementStore`); the **paywall UI shipped in 6.2** (`PaywallSheet`, honest, annual default, Restore + legal); the **value-gate triggers wired in 6.3** — a gated Convert (free batch > limit) or a locked option (File size / Strip) now presents the paywall at the moment of need, emits `pro_gate_hit`, and resumes the blocked action on purchase. The real entitlement flows from `EntitlementStore` into `ConvertViewModel`, so Pro users (including at launch) see no locks and no gate; the paywall also still has its permanent home in Settings.

### Products (task 6.1 — fetched from RevenueCat offerings, prices read from the store at runtime)
| Product | Package type | Price | Grants |
|---------|------|-------|--------|
| Pro Annual | Annual | $9.99 | `pro` entitlement |
| Pro Weekly | Weekly | $1.99 | `pro` entitlement |
| Pro Lifetime | Lifetime | $19.99 | `pro` entitlement |

> All three unlock the single **`HeicSwap Pro`** entitlement (that exact string is the RevenueCat entitlement *identifier* — `PurchaseService.entitlementIdentifier` must match it verbatim, since `customerInfo.entitlements` is keyed by identifier). The app reads whatever the **current** offering exposes (no hardcoded prices); App Store Connect + RevenueCat dashboard must be configured to match (sandbox now, live in 11.1). Without an API key, `PurchaseService` no-ops safely and the cached entitlement stands.

### Paywall Strategy
Honest paywall (annual default, restore, no dark patterns), built in 6.2 (`PaywallSheet`). Free tier: basic conversion + small PDF up to ~5 items. `EntitlementStore.isPro` is the gate signal `ConvertViewModel` reads (synced via the view); `EntitlementStore.products`/`purchase`/`restore` feed the paywall. Terms link uses Apple's Standard EULA; the privacy URL is a placeholder (`LegalLinks.privacyPolicy`) pending the hosted policy (task 11.1). Presentation triggers from gated Pro actions (Convert over the free batch limit, locked File-size/Strip options) are wired in 6.3, each presenting the paywall at the moment of need and resuming on purchase. Settings (8.1) adds a **Restore** button (re-syncs entitlements via `EntitlementStore.restore`) and an App-Store **Manage Subscription** link beside the paywall entry; the strip-by-default control in Settings is itself Pro-gated for free users.
